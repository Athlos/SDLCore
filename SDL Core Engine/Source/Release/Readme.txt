		CIRCUIT BREAKERS - Pascal Cross 1301653

	CONTROLS


Q to show controls ingame

W to show prices ingame

F3 to quit


	BASIC MECHANICS

S to start the next wave

SPACE to pause the game

Click on enemies to shoot them, clicking on towers upgrades them if you have the money

1 or 2 to spawn either a rail or beam tower, beam is better but more expensive

F to sell the tower you hover over


	ABILITIES

R to spawn mine where you hover over

A to start slow motion, lasts 10 seconds, can stack


	UPGRADING

Click on tower to upgrade it

D to upgrade click damage

E to upgrade wall

T to repair wall


	CHEATS

F1 to give yourself electricity

F2 to increase the wave counter
